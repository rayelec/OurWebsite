Who is RAYELEC

Our company's story began in the 1970s, when the company founder and our loving father Mr Brahma Dev Ray started this business from a small shop in Sundargarh.

With a strong foundation of dedication and hard work, we have proudly become a three-generation family-run business, maintaining the same values of reliability and quality that our founder instilled in us. Our commitment to serving our customers with integrity and excellence runs deep, and we are honored to continue this legacy for years to come.
